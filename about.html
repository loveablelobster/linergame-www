About LinerGame

The company LinerGame was established in 2014 to offer a range of unique educational products and training services. The primary users are the global liner shipping industry and related businesses as well as relevant educational institutions. We emphasize hands-on and simulation based training methods brought into play by a team of dedicated trainers / facilitators with significant expertise on industry matters.

We offer a range of standard as well as bespoke training solutions matching current and future training needs of our clients. Our mission is to equip the workforce of our clients with the knowledge, skills and collaborative values required to compete in the future where the industry will undergo tough market fundamentals and be exposed to disruptive business models due to increased digitalization.

I learned a lot from playing the liner shipping game and I learned it fast. It helps you understand the complexities of coordinating between countries and ports and between key personnel groups. The scoring system allows you to understand revenue and cost tradeoffs – for instance incurring a small cost in one port means that you can avoid a large cost in the next. Overall it is a highly valuable learning aid.
- Chris Ellegaard, Professor, Aarhus University
